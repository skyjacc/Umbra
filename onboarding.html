UMBRA·EQ Getting started

A live 11-band equalizer and bass boost for any browser tab. No ads, no tracking, works fully offline.

  1. Open a tab that is playing sound (music, video, a call).
  2. Click the Umbra EQ icon in the toolbar to open the panel.
  3. Press EQ This Tab — that tab is now equalized live.
  4. Drag the dots to shape the sound. To EQ another tab, open it and press EQ This Tab again.

Good to know

  • Shift-drag a dot changes its width (Q); double-click resets that band.
  • The strip on the left of the graph is master volume.
  • Presets save your settings and sync across your browser.
  • Spectrum shows the live frequency graph — it is visual only.
  • Loud audio can harm hearing and speakers — keep it sensible.
