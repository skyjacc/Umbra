UmbraEQ
Loud audio can harm hearing — keep it sensible

Quick start

Open a tab that plays sound, then press EQ This Tab. That tab is now equalized. To EQ another tab, open it and press EQ This Tab again.

Shape the sound

Drag a dot: left/right = frequency, up/down = boost/cut. Shift-drag = width (Q). Double-click resets a band. The strip on the left is master volume.

Presets & spectrum

Save or apply presets from Presets (they sync across your browser). Spectrum draws the live frequency graph — visual only, it does not change the sound.
