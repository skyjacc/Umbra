Umbra EQ

A live 11-band equalizer & bass boost for any browser tab — 100% local, no tracking.

Quick start
1

Play some audio

Open any tab with sound — music, a video, a stream or a call.

2

Turn it on

Click the Umbra EQ icon in the toolbar, then press EQ This Tab. That tab is now equalized.

3

Shape the sound

Drag the dots on the curve to boost or cut any frequency, live. The strip on the left is master volume.

Shift-drag a dot = change width
Double-click a dot = reset it
Presets — save, apply & share by code
Rules — auto-apply your sound per site
Every tab keeps its own EQ
Spectrum — see what you're hearing

Your audio never leaves your computer. Umbra processes sound on your device and never records, stores, or sends it anywhere — no account, no ads, no tracking.
